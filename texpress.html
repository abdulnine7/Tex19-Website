TEXPRESS

Details

Max. No. Of Participants: 2 per team

Registration Fees: Rs 200/- per team

No. of Rounds: 1

INTRODUCTION:

Love expressing through writing? Or are you someone who thinks a picture is worth a thousand words? Well this event is for both of you. Come experience the complete event with us and be a part of this wonderful venture.

EVENT DETAILS:

  • Each team will be a part of TEXEPHYR’19 for both the days and will document all the events taking place over the course of two days.
  • At the end of the event, participants are supposed to create a full sized newspaper page article, either written or digital and submit their entries.
  • They are allowed to click pictures and use the same in their reports/articles.
  • Winners will be announced on the website (www.texephyr.in) within 2 days of event completion.

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA:

  • Articles would be judged on the basis of their accuracy and presentation.
  • Judgement won’t be biased on the quality of images. Photos clicked with a phone camera will be treated the same as the ones clicked with a DSLR.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:

  • Any student with valid ID card of their educational institute can participate.

** Prize money may be subjected to change. Organisers reserve the right to change any rules.

Texephyr'19

Texephyr is MIT-WPU's National Level Technical Fest.This event has been inspiring the minds of young students, igniting them to conquer the challenges faced today and propose innovating solutions that will flow a fresh wind of change for development of society as well as themselves.
