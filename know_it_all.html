KNOW IT ALL

Details

Max. No. Of Participants: 2

Registration Fees:

  • Rs 120/- (SAE member participants)
  • Rs 180/- (non SAE member participants)

No. of Rounds: 3

INTRODUCTION:

For all those who want to see their ideas stand up tall! This event is a perfect blend of acknowledgement and analyzation of a concept. The event will mainly test your knowledge of the subjects namely SOM and applied mechanics. The final round will be based on your ability to apply your acquired knowledge.

EVENT FORMAT:

  • ROUND I (Elimination Round):
    • In this round, there will be a knowledge test consisting of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ).
    • MCQs will be based on Engineering Mechanics and Strength of material. Test duration will be 40 minutes.
  • ROUND II:
    • Teams which will crack Round 1 will compete in this rapid fire round.
    • In this round, participants have to identify various logos and images related to mechanical and automobile engineering.
    • Judgement will be done on the basis of number of objects identified.
    • This is an elimination round. Top teams will qualify for the next round.
    • The machine must be propelled forward using IC engines (Max power of 4 CC). But use of any other chemicals, compressed gas, rocket powered systems, DC motors or any other means as found dangerous by the organizers will not be allowed.
    • Start control mechanism: The start control mechanism must not provide any thrust to the vehicle along direction of motion of track.
    • Those participants using IC engines must note that their starting mechanism must be removable and not interfere with the race.
    • The vehicle will be inspected beforehand and will be disqualified if found dangerous by the judges.
    • The participants may add some innovative mechanisms (Reverse Mechanism) in their car so as to get bonus points.
  • ROUND III (Final Round):
    • Teams will be given a practical industrial problem statement and they are supposed to work out a practical solution for the given problem.
    • A time limit of 30 minutes will be set for this task.
  • Judgement Criteria:
    • Judgement will be based on the most efficient as well as cost effective solution.
    • Teams providing the most convenient and fastest solution wins.
    • Decision of the judges will be final.
  • ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
    • Any student with valid ID card of their educational institute can participate.

** Prize money may be subjected to change. Organisers reserve the right to change any rules.
