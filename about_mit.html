MAEER'S Maharashtra Institute of Technology (MIT), established in 1983, is today amongst the top engineering colleges in India. The news magazine 'Outlook' has ranked MIT Pune as 1st in Maharashtra && 11th in India amongst the finest Private Engineering Colleges. MIT is affiliated to the University of Pune and recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi.

MIT, Pune is the prominent institute of Maharashtra Academy of Engineering and Education Research (MAAER’s) Group of Institutions, spread across the country with more than sixty other academic institutions. In 2017, MIT went through a transition to became MIT-World Peace University (MIT-WPU). MIT-WPU now continues the legacy of MIT and is the seat of Peace, Research, Social and National Initiatives. It boasts of tie-ups with universities and industries abroad to ensure global education to its students. MIT-WPU promotes several academic and extra curricular activities for benefit of students at large.
